First: Why is this important to address?

Post Traumatic Stress Disorder or PTSD is a mental disorder that affects a great number of individuals around the U.S. In fact, as many as 35,574 people in 2024 were considered homeless as a result of this unaddressed mental issue that is a result of damaging war experiences. Because many of them are senior citizens, providing them with adequate resources and shelter is morally crucial.

What can you do?

There are many ways you can support our homeless veterans. One of the easiest way is to donate personal items such as clothing, food, or make cash contributions. Volunteering at local shelters and organizations that support veterans can also be another great way to support those in need.
